Master of Science Psychotherapy (Global Open University of Nagaland)

They have used Sensory Integration Therapy and Neuro Developmental Therapy in USA before migrating to India. This is when Nikhil Bole learnt under their tutelage, precious techniques to treat learning problems and Autism, ADHD, ADD etc. Although it is widely believed that these problems cannot be cured completely, Nikhil differs in his perception and aims to achieve 90% or more Normalcy for his patients.
